Three Easy Steps to be a part of our family

01

Select a style you want to master

Start by deciding what style of dance you want to learn. There are tons out there — individual dance styles like ballet, jazz, and hip hop, and partner styles like ballroom and salsa which will figure out what type you're interested in before you get started, which will make it easier to find the best resources.

02

Decide on technique or routine

decide whether you want to learn basic technique or a specific dance. Most dance styles have foundational movements that trained dancers practice repeatedly to build their strength, endurance, and skill.

03

Do the lessons and practice

They say it takes 10,000 hours to master a new skill — don't give up! Keep practicing with the same video and new videos with other teachers to get better at your dance. You'll see improvement the more you work at it.
